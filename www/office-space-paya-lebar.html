Office Space Paya Lebar

Paya Lebar Square centre is a commercial development with a 10 storey premium-grade office tower and 2 wings. It also has 3 storey retail podium, that together offer a great quality of work-life balance and convenient location for all types of businesses. Many of the buildings units offer floor-to-ceiling natural light that offer bright working spaces and enjoy panoramic views of the city skyline.

Paya Lebar Square is located right above Paya Lebar MRT Interchange and has an excellent frontage along Paya Lebar Road, with the Central Business District (CBD), Changi Airport and Orchard Road shopping belt just 10 to 15 minutes’ drive away. Established developments near Paya Lebar Square include the Singapore Post Centre, Certis CISCO Centre and future integrated projects along Sims Avenue/Tanjong Katong Road as well as the Lion City Hotel Redevelopment.
